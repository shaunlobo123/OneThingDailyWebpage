How it works

Nurturing habits, day by day.

A simple, structured daily ritual that builds personal consistency and shared momentum.

Step 1

Set Your Daily Focus

Every morning, decide on the single wholesome action you will accomplish today. Write it down directly to set your intention. No cluttered, overwhelming todo lists—just one meaningful task.

"What is your goal for today? e.g., Read 10 pages, buy your mum flowers..."
Step 2

Capture Visual Proof

Once complete, take a quick photo inside the app. Whether it's your open textbook, a nature walk, or a fresh meal, snap real-life confirmation of your victory.

Step 3

Seal the Commitment

Tap and hold anywhere on the screen to verify your wholesome action. A progress animation completes the task and provides orbs to grow the canaopy.

Step 4

Connect Privately

Your post goes straight to a feed shared strictly with close friends. View their daily victories, send reactions, and encourage each other. No algorithm, No vanity metrics. Just mutual support and encouragement.

Step 5

Grow Your Canopy

Watch your wholesome choices shape a physical tree. Each completed task grows a new leaf on your tree canopy, creating a golden, visual representation of your discipline over time. Unlock new trees every month with new themes in the future.

"One good thing a day, grown into a forest of triumphs."
